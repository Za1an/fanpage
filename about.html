Teen Titans is a television cartoon show that aired from July 19, 2003 to January 16, 2006. It was a cartoon adaptation of the Teen Titans comic books of the late '80s, and is by far the most popular adaptation of the Titans. It stars the Teen Titans, which consist of Robin, Beastboy, Cyborg, Starfire, and Raven. The show focused on the heroes and the hubris' of each characters: Robin's overwhelming care for his friends, Beastboy's feeling of being inadequacy, Starfire's immigration from a different planet, Cyborg's struggle with being half robot, and Raven's "daddy issues."
